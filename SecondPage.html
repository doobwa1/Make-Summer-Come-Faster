2 Simple Ways to Make Summer Come Faster



Make a Summer Bucket List

Summer Bucket Lists are a common thing that teens make. If you are unfamiliar with what this list is, here is how you make one. Making a Bucket List is quite easy. You just write down a bunch of fun things that you want to do this summer. Make sure that they are things that are pretty reasonable. For example, getting really tan, or visitig every ice cream shop in a beach town. The next step is to make your Bucket List pretty! You can decorate and color your list and add any designs you want. Then, hang it up in your room or somehwere where you will see it everyday. You can focus on thinking about and planning all of the things and activites on your list, which will definitly make the time fly by!! The final step is to complete your Summer Bucket List!!!
